About Us

Typing kids is an interactive typing game for kids. Through the course interactive lessons, kids will learn all about the alphabet and practice sight words, word families, and simple sentences. Aimed at students in pre-k, kindergarten, and the 1st grade, Typing kids will also teach children to associate each letter on the keyboard with the correct finger while improving alphabet recognition, dexterity, and familiarity with technology.

Engaging Videos

Our engaging videos keep kids entertained with friendly characters and music while helping them learn the shape and sound of each letter, warming up their hands, and getting them ready to type by associating each finger with letters on the keyboard.
